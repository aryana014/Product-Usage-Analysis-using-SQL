Project Goal:
Understand how users move through the product and where they drop off.

Data Used:
User signups and action events generated as sample data.

Key Insights:
Most users drop after viewing features.
Only a small number reach later actions.

Hypotheses:
Users may not see clear next steps.
Onboarding flow may be confusing.

Recommendations:
Improve onboarding clarity.
Add clearer call-to-action prompts.
